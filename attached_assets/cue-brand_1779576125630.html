Construction

Anatomy
Ring + tail = Q. The tail always runs the lower-right diagonal at 45°, breaking past the ring like a true Q descender.
  • Inner dot stays centered — it's the live cue signal and the focal glow.
  • Gradient flows top-left violet → bottom-right teal; never recolor mid-stroke.
  • Use the mono cut on busy or single-color backgrounds; keep clear space ≥ the dot's diameter.
  • Wordmark is Bricolage Grotesque 700, tight tracking, lowercase-friendly.
